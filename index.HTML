United States Navy SEALS,
The best special force in the world.

The United States Navy Sea, Air, and Land (SEAL) Teams,
commonly known as Navy SEALs,
are the U.S. Navy's primary special operations force
and a component of the Naval Special Warfare Command.
Among the SEALs' main functions are conducting small-unit
special operation missions in maritime, jungle,
urban, arctic, mountainous, and desert environments.
SEALs are typically ordered to capture or to eliminate high level targets
, or to gather intelligence behind enemy lines.
